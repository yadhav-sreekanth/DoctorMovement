What We Offer

Expert Therapies
for Every Need

From acute injuries to chronic pain — we have the right treatment to restore your function and quality of life.

01
Taping

Kinesio and rigid taping to support muscles and joints, reduce pain, and accelerate recovery without restricting movement.

02
Cupping Therapy

Traditional cupping techniques enhanced with modern physiotherapy to improve circulation, reduce muscle tension, and relieve pain.

03
Dry Needling

Targeted trigger point release using fine needles to treat myofascial pain, muscle tightness, and dysfunction effectively.

04
Manual Therapy

Hands-on techniques including joint mobilisation, manipulation, and soft tissue therapy to restore mobility and reduce pain.

05
Electrotherapy

Advanced electrotherapy modalities including TENS, ultrasound, and IFT for pain management and tissue healing.

06
Homecare & Online

Full physiotherapy services at home, plus online video consultations so you get expert care wherever you are in Kerala.
